Welcome

St. Louis Pet Rescue is a non-profit rescue group focusing on saving pets from local animal control facilities and neglect situations.

STLPR does not have a facility, nor any paid staff. STLPR relies on the generosity of others to volunteer their time to help pets in need. Read more about how you can help.
